Citizen Scientist Fall 2022 Hackathon
Home Rules

    • Welcome to the Citizen Scientist's Fall 2022 Hackathon!

    Rules

    • Participation

      • You will be assigned to a small team (3 - 5 persons) to create a design for the Citizen Scientist's flood reporting webpage. Be sure to include some UI features such as to ensure ease of usability for users.

    • Submission

      • Please put a link or .pdf of your submission (whatever format you chose to design with) within the #submissions channel on the Discord. Please ensure that you include your team number and names when posting your submission. Please include a small paragraph with your post, describing your submission and the reasoning behind your design. This will allow the judges to better understand your design choices.

    • Due Date

      • Please ensure that you post your submission by 23:59 November 20th EST

    • Resources

      • Please see the Home page as well as the #resources channel on Discord to find some potentially useful resources to help with your project.

    • Judging Guidelines

      • The Judges will be judging your project based upon the user accessibility, consistency, functionality, and aesthetics of your design.
